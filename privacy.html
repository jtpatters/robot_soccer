Privacy Policy

App: Robot Soccer 3D · Last updated: May 8, 2026

Short version: Robot Soccer 3D does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything happens on your device, offline.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, device identifiers, contacts, location, photos, microphone input, camera input, browsing activity, advertising identifiers, or any other information about you or your device.

Accounts and sign-in

The app has no accounts. There is no sign-in, no profile, no email or password, no social login, and no link to any external service.

Network use

The app plays entirely offline. It does not contact any server, third-party service, ad network, or analytics provider. After installation it does not need an internet connection at all.

On-device storage

The app saves a small amount of game progress locally on your iPad so that your earned stars persist between sessions. This information stays on your device and is never sent anywhere. It includes only:

  • Best star rating (1–3 ⭐) earned for each level
  • A few player preferences (animation speed, button size)

You can clear this data at any time from inside the game (the dev panel contains a “reset all stars” button) or by deleting and reinstalling the app.

Third-party services

None. The app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, crash reporters, advertising networks, or tracking technologies of any kind.

In-app purchases and ads

There are no in-app purchases. There are no advertisements.

Children's privacy

Robot Soccer 3D is designed to be safe for children of all ages. Because we do not collect or transmit any data, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). The app complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including its provisions for children.

Your rights

Because no personal data is collected or stored off-device, there is nothing for us to access, export, correct, or delete on your behalf. To remove all locally-stored game progress, simply delete the app from your iPad.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will also be reflected in the app's listing on the App Store.
